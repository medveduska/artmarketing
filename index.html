About

The lens behind
Malta's best brands

Arthur is a Maltese creative director with over a decade of experience capturing the energy, identity and story of brands that want to stand out. From the atmospheric interiors of Valletta's finest restaurants to the electric atmosphere of Malta's biggest nightclub events — every project is a visual strategy, not just a shoot.

artmarketing works closely with restaurants, bars, nightclubs and event organisers across Malta and the wider European market — including event brands like Gumbal — delivering photography, video and web that convert audiences into loyal customers.
